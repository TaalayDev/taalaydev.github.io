Welcome to PocketPet Companion! Your trust is the foundation of our friendship. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with your AI pet. We are committed to being transparent about our practices so you can focus on the fun.

In a Nutshell: We only collect data essential for your PocketPet to grow, learn, and chat with you. We will never sell your personal data. Your conversations are private, and you are in control.

üìäInformation We Collect

Virtual Pet & Game Data

  • Pet Profile: Information you provide about your pet (name, species, etc.).
  • Interaction Logs: Data about how you care for your pet (feeding, playing, cleaning).
  • Customization Choices: Items, outfits, and decorations you unlock and use.
  • Game Progress: Scores and achievements from in-app mini-games.

AI Chat Data

  • Conversation History: Transcripts of your chats with your AI companion to maintain context and improve their memory.
  • User Inputs: Questions, statements, and topics you discuss.
  • Processing Note: Chat data is sent to our secure AI partners to generate responses. We do not use your private conversations to train public AI models.

Technical & Usage Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique identifiers needed for app functionality and security.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data about which features you use most, helping us improve the app experience for everyone.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous reports that help us find and fix bugs faster.

üéØHow We Use Your Information

To Provide the Core Experience

  • To enable your PocketPet to grow and develop a unique personality.
  • To power the AI chat, allowing your companion to remember past conversations and respond intelligently.
  • To save your game progress, customizations, and rewards.
  • To sync your data across devices if you choose to create an account.

To Improve and Personalize

  • Analyzing anonymized usage data to understand which features are popular and where we can improve.
  • Fixing bugs, enhancing performance, and ensuring app stability.
  • Developing new games, features, and customization options based on community feedback.
Your Conversations are Private: Your chat history is used to enhance *your* specific companion's memory and conversational ability. It is not used for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

ü§ùData Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers. Ever.

We only share data with trusted partners for essential functions:

  • AI Service Providers: We send your chat inputs to our AI partners (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) to generate your companion's responses. They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: We use secure cloud services (like Google Firebase) for data backup, account syncing, and hosting our services.
  • Analytics Services: We share anonymized, aggregated usage data with analytics providers to help us understand app performance.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.

üîíData Security and Retention

Our Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers.
  • Your data is encrypted at rest in our secure cloud databases.
  • Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who need it to perform their jobs.

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Pet & Game Data: Retained as long as your account is active, or until you decide to delete it.
  • Chat History: Stored to provide conversational memory. You can clear your chat history at any time within the app's settings.
  • Inactive Accounts: Data associated with accounts that have been inactive for over 2 years may be automatically deleted.
