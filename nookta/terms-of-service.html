Privacy Policy

Nookta  ·  Last updated: June 18, 2026  ·  Effective date: June 18, 2026

Short version: Nookta stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

1. Who We Are

Nookta ("the App", "we", "us") is a business inventory and management application developed by Taalaybek Aslanbek uulu. The App is available on macOS, iOS, and Windows.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Nookta is an offline-first application. All data you enter — including business names, product inventories, financial records, and customer information — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database.

We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or contact details
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files beyond what you explicitly choose to import
  • Use analytics SDKs or advertising trackers
  • Transmit any business data to our servers

3. Data Storage

All application data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. The location of this database is managed by the operating system's standard application data directory. You have full control over this data at all times.

Backup and restore functionality (if used) writes data to files you choose on your own device or personal cloud storage (e.g. iCloud Drive, OneDrive). We have no access to those files.

4. Camera Access

The App may request access to your device's camera solely for scanning barcodes. Camera access is used in real time and no images are stored or transmitted. You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device's system settings.

5. File Access

When you use import or export features, the App may request access to specific files you select. Only files you explicitly choose are read. No files are uploaded to any server.

6. Third-Party Services

Nookta does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services. The App does not contain any tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

Nookta is a business management tool intended for users aged 17 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

8. Data Security

Because all data stays on your device, security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your operating system and the App up to date.

9. Your Rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on our end. To remove all App data, simply delete the App from your device — this removes the local database entirely.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
