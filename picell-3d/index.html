Core features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🎨

2D-First Workflow

Draw in a pixel art canvas you already understand. The 3D model generates automatically from your artwork — no 3D modeling experience required.

✦

Materials & Bloom

Assign Solid, Emissive, Neon, Metal, or Glass per color. Neon/Emissive get real-time UnrealBloom post-processing for stunning glow effects.

📦

5 Export Formats

PNG spritesheet, OBJ+MTL for Blender, GLB for Unity, VOX for MagicaVoxel, JSON for custom pipelines. All from the Render Studio.

🔲

6 View Projections

Edit from all 6 sides — Front, Back, Left, Right, Top, Bottom. All views share the same voxel grid. Depth-aware with signed offset labels.

🖼️

Image Import

Convert any PNG to pixel art instantly. Or import semi-transparent reference overlays — drag, resize, and adjust opacity while you trace.

⚡

Shape Tools

Circle, ellipse, rectangle, and an editable polyline. The line tool stays live — drag handles to bend it, press Enter to commit.

Simple workflow

From pixel to 3D in minutes.

01

Draw in 2D

Open the canvas, pick colors, draw with pencil, shapes, or flood fill. Exactly like any pixel art editor.

02

Set Depth

Adjust the depth slider and direction — Both, Front, or Back. Watch the 3D preview update live with every stroke.

03

Apply Materials

Assign Neon, Metal, Glass, or Emissive per color. The bloom post-processing responds in real time.

04

Export

Open Render Studio. Choose camera angle, lighting preset, adjust bloom, then download in your format of choice.
