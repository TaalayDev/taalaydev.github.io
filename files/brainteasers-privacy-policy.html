Privacy Policy for BrainTeasers

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application BrainTeasers (the "Application"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Application, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

BrainTeasers is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information, and the app functions primarily without network connectivity. We may collect:

  • Game Progress Information: We store your game progress, scores, and achievements locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device may be collected for crash reporting and analytics purposes, such as device model, operating system version, and app performance data.
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous data about how you use the Application, such as which puzzles you play, time spent on puzzles, and general interaction patterns.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To save your game progress and achievements
  • To improve and optimize the Application's performance and user experience
  • To identify and fix bugs or issues within the Application
  • To analyze usage patterns and enhance our puzzle offerings

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is primarily stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, or destruction.

Offline Functionality

BrainTeasers is designed to function fully offline. Your progress is saved locally on your device, allowing you to play without an internet connection.

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools to help us measure traffic and usage trends for the Application. These tools collect information sent by your device, including the puzzles you access and time spent on those puzzles, to help us improve our offering.

Children's Privacy

Our Application is suitable for people of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us so we can promptly remove the information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. You can also clear local application data through your device settings, which will reset your game progress.
